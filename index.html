Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pokémon Datenbank?

This website allows you to browse and filter Pokémon by type and category. You can also add, edit, and manage your own collection of Pokémon.

How can I filter Pokémon?

Click on the "Kategorien" button to select a category, and then the Pokémon list will filter based on the category you choose (e.g., Feuer, Wasser, etc.).

How do I contact you?

You can contact us by filling out the form in the "Contact Us" section or by emailing us directly at contact@yourwebsite.com.
